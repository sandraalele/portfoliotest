04 — Work Samples

Real work, real results

A look at how I approach each area — with the tools and systems I use daily to deliver results.

Task & Project Boards

Turning to-do lists into structured, visual workflows

I help teams stop wondering “what’s next?” by building clear, visual project workspaces. I work fluently across both Trello and ClickUp — choosing the right tool for each team’s way of working.

Trello

Workflow Architecture: Stages built as To-Do → In Progress → Review → Done so anyone can see where a project stands instantly.
Smart Labels: Custom priority tags (Urgent, Normal) so teams always know what needs attention first.
Card Detail: Checklists, key milestones, descriptions, and activity logs on every card for full accountability.
Progress Tracking: Weekly check-in views to summarize completions and upcoming work.

ClickUp

Board View: Kanban columns (To Do, Planning, In Progress, At Risk) for visual team coordination.
List View: Granular task tracking with assignees, due dates, and priority flags for each deliverable.
Dashboard View: Executive-level summary with task counts, workload by status, and progress charts for stakeholder reporting.
Subtask Breakdown: Every task broken into clear subtasks so execution stays focused and on track.
The Result: Less “where are we on this?” emails and more time spent on high-level execution. You get a bird’s-eye view of your business while I handle the ground-level details.

Email Management

Think of me as your inbox’s personal air traffic controller

I manage and organize inboxes to ensure timely, professional communication while eliminating clutter. Through strategic filtering, priority systems, and efficient processing, I help maintain inbox zero.

Inbox Organization: Custom labels for Appointments, Client Inquiries, Follow-ups, Newsletters, and Personal — every email in its place.
Smart Filters: Auto-routing rules so emails are sorted the moment they arrive — no manual sorting needed.
Vacation Responses: Out-of-office messages set up to maintain professionalism and manage expectations while you’re away.
Response Templates: Professionally drafted replies for common client interactions and follow-ups.
Priority Flagging: Urgent emails like conversion drops and contract renewals are surfaced immediately for your attention.
The Result: A 60% reduction in time spent on daily email triage and a guaranteed response time for your VIP clients. Instead of starting your day with 1,000+ unread messages, you open a clean, organised inbox where the most important emails are already highlighted.

Calendar & Schedule Management

A calendar should be a roadmap, not a cage

Managing a calendar is more than just avoiding double-bookings. It’s about ensuring you have the mental space to lead, the time to create, and the freedom to disconnect. I act as the gatekeeper of your most valuable resource: your time.

Time Blocking & Batching: I organize your day into logical flows, grouping meetings together to preserve “Deep Work” blocks for your most important projects.
Buffer Management: 30-minute recovery buffers built around every meeting — no more back-to-back fatigue.
Automated Workflows: Using Calendly, I streamline booking with strict “Rules of Engagement” (e.g., max 4 calls per day, no Monday morning meetings).
Multi-Client Coordination: Managing meetings across multiple contacts and time zones simultaneously — seamlessly.
Recurring Rituals: Daily morning rituals, weekly planning sessions, and team check-ins are protected and consistent.
The Result: Instead of starting your day overwhelmed, you open a clean calendar where your most important work is already protected and your meetings are spaced for maximum focus.
